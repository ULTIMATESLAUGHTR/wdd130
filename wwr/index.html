Want to Experience the Best White-Water Rafting in Utah? Look No Further!

Welcome to the Wild Rapids!

We are the best white-water rafting company in Utah. We have been in business for over 20 years and have the best guides in the industry. We offer a variety of trips for all skill levels, from beginners to experts. Our trips are designed to be fun, safe, and memorable.

The Wild Rapids specialize in custom trips during the Spring/Summer Seasons. You can enjoy peace of mind while our Trip Planners help sort out the details so you can have the best experience alone or with family/friends. If you're ready to begin planning a trip with us, scroll down further on the page or use the "Contact Us" button to get our phone number and email options to reach us as soon as possible. Spots are limited so a reservation is highly recommended.

We are located in Ogden, Utah, where we focus on rafting in the Ogden River. Our trips are all-inclusive, so you can focus on just having fun! Our staff will provide all equipment based on your needs.
