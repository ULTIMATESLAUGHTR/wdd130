The Wild Rapids Trip and Reservation Details

Trip Summary

Our first, and most popular is our Half-Day Adventure offering. Time Slots are limited to mornings from 8AM-12PM(Noon).

Our second offering is our Full-Day Adventure. Aimed towards Professional Rafters. Time Slots are open from 8AM-7PM [Weather and Sunlight Permitting].

What to Expect from your Trip

After a quick safety check on equipment and rafting procedures our team will meet you at the Ogden River. After embarking, every 1 hour we'll do a wellness check to ensure everyone can continue rafting. Lunchtime will start at the 3 or 4 hour mark depending on guests. (food not provided).

Similar to the half-day adventure schedule, with breaks to eat and rest as needed. Lunchtime can also be scheduled earlier or later depending on preferences. After 5PM, weather depending we'll suggest an ending point to finish the trip, or if plans have already been established to continue for up to 2 hours more for evening low-light rafting practice. (For Certified Rafting Experts Only).
